==================================================
CORE PROBLEMS TO FIX
==================================================

The site is structurally solid, but the public-facing conversion layer needs improvement.

Fix these issues:

1. Hero section is too weak
- It needs a stronger value proposition
- It should immediately communicate:
  - what the business does
  - who it is for
  - starting price
  - clear CTA

2. The site is not targeting the audience strongly enough
- It should speak directly to:
  - hairstylists
  - barbers
  - beauty brands
  - small local businesses

3. Pricing section lacks hierarchy
- The $300 Business Website package should feel like the best and most recommended option

4. Portfolio copy needs stronger impact
- The descriptions should feel more premium and more result-focused
- The demos should feel like desirable examples, not just placeholders

5. Trust is too light
- Add a trust-building section with reasons to choose Books and Brews

6. Closing CTA needs more urgency and clarity
- Add a stronger final call to action near the bottom of the homepage

7. Quote page needs clearer expectation-setting
- Mention price range naturally
- Make it feel low-pressure and easy to submit
